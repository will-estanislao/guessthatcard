What Are Zener Cards?

Telekinesis, telepathy, and clairvoyance. Extrasensory perception beyond the 5 physical senses of taste, touch, smell, sight and hearing. Is it possible to measure psychic abilities?

In the 1930s, J.B. Rhine attempted to measure ESP through experiments. He used Zener cards to test ones psychic abilities, specifically telepathy.

The experiment consists of a sender and receiver. The sender views the cards, while the receiver guesses the symbols.

However, measuring psychic abilities with Zener cards proved to be inconclusive. Subjects were able to cheat the test by seeing reading the experimenter's expressions, discerning symbols through indentation on the back of cards, or through a reflection.

ESP and paranormal research are classified as pseudosciences.

Symbols

The Zener card designs are made up of a circle, cross, wave, square and a star. A deck of Zener cards are made up of 25 cards with 5 of each symbol. The name of the cards originates from Karl Zener, a perceptual psychologist who worked with J.B. Rhine.

In Popular Media

Ghostbusters(1984), Peter Venkman tests a pair of volunteers using Zener cards.
